We are The Tenth Wall

Redefining the space between imagination and reality.

Our Story

The Tenth Wall was born from a vision to go beyond conventional interior design. Our founders believed a home isn’t defined by its four walls, but by the feelings and dreams it holds.

The name The Tenth Wall symbolizes the unseen layer — the personality, energy, and individuality of the people who inhabit a space.

Today, we’re proud to have transformed 200+ spaces across cities, maintaining our commitment to personal, story-driven design that inspires.

Our Design Philosophy

At The Tenth Wall, we believe in storytelling through space. Every design we create is rooted in emotion, functionality, and timeless style.

Whether it's clean and minimal, lush and green, or creatively layered — our approach remains client-first and context-driven. We don’t just style; we sculpt your lifestyle into space.

Minimalist

Simplicity with purpose. Clean lines, open space, and intentional accents.

Biophilic

Nature-inspired design that brings in calm, light, and wellness.

Eclectic

A curated mix of textures, tones, and tales that reflect personality.

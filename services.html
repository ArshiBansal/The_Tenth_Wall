Design that speaks your story

From full-scale interiors to personalized consultations,

The Tenth Wall offers a wide range of design services tailored just for you.

What We Offer

Interior Design

Our interior design solutions are crafted to reflect your lifestyle, aspirations, and comfort. We mix creativity with functionality to transform any space into a personal haven.

From minimalist studios to luxurious residences, our designs are timeless, detail-oriented, and always client-centric. Every wall, corner, and color is thoughtfully planned.

We guide you from concept development to final installation—ensuring a seamless and aesthetic space you’ll love returning to every day.

Lighting

Lighting can completely change the mood and perception of a space. Our lighting designs are layered, practical, and artistic—enhancing every nook with elegance.

We offer a variety of lighting plans—ambient, task, and accent—that are tailored to each room’s needs. Be it cozy reading corners or lively social spaces—we light it right.

Our solutions integrate the latest in energy-efficient, smart, and automated lighting systems, ensuring style meets sustainability.

Renovation & Remodeling

Upgrade your existing space with our end-to-end renovation and remodeling services. We specialize in kitchens, bathrooms, living rooms, and more.

Our process begins with understanding your pain points and goals. We modernize your space with improved layouts, textures, and updated finishes.

From structural revamps to aesthetic upgrades, we ensure the project is completed efficiently, safely, and beautifully—within your timeline and budget.

Space Planning

We create intuitive floor plans and space layouts that improve flow, purpose, and functionality—whether it’s a 1BHK flat or a multi-level villa.

Each plan is tailored to your lifestyle—balancing storage, privacy, light, and openness. We carefully zone every area to avoid wasted square footage.

Our 2D and 3D drafts let you visualize layouts before execution, giving you full control and clarity on how your space will work for you.

Consultation

If you’re unsure where to begin, our design consultations offer clarity and expert advice. Whether it’s a room makeover or a full renovation—we’re here to help.

We listen to your needs, review your space, and suggest actionable steps tailored to your goals, budget, and preferences. You get professional insights and ideas in just one session.

From material selection to design direction, our consultations empower you to take confident, informed decisions at every step of your project.

Our Process

1. Initial Consultation
